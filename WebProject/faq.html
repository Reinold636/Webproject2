Frequently Asked Questions

1. What skin types can use your products?

Our products are made to be safe and helpful for all kinds of skin. Whether your skin is oily, dry, mixed, or sensitive, you can use our products without worry.

2. Are your products tested on animals?

No, we do not test any of our products on animals. We love animals and want to make sure our products are cruelty-free.

3. Do your products have bad chemicals?

No! We do not use harmful chemicals like parabens or sulfates in our products. Our ingredients are gentle and safe for your skin.

4. How long will it take to see changes in my skin?

If you use our products every day, you can usually see good changes in your skin in about 2 to 4 weeks. Everyoneâ€™s skin is different, so results may vary.

5. Can people with acne use your products?

Yes! Our products are made not to block pores and are safe for people with acne. They help your skin stay clean and healthy without making acne worse.

6. Can pregnant women use your products?

Most of our products are safe to use during pregnancy. But to be extra safe, it is a good idea to talk to your doctor before using any skincare products.

7. How should I keep my skincare products?

Keep your products in a cool, dry place, away from the sun. This will help keep them fresh and working well for a long time.

8. Do you have small sample sizes I can try first?

Yes, we have small sample sizes for some of our products. You can check our website to see which ones are available for samples.

9. How can I know where my order is?

When your order is sent, we will send you a tracking number by email. You can use that number to check the status of your package anytime online.

10. What if I want to return a product?

If you are not happy with your purchase, you can return unopened products within 30 days. Please visit our Returns page for all the details on how to return your order.
